About TurboGuard
================

TurboGuard is an advanced time series anomaly detection toolkit designed to monitor and predict engine health using state-of-the-art LSTM-based models.  
Our mission is to provide a reliable, easy-to-use platform for predictive maintenance and anomaly detection in industrial applications.
